🛡️ Authentication Factors
1. Knowledge
"What you know"		 Passwords or PINs. The weakest theoretical factor because they can be easily guessed, socially engineered, or stolen globally via database leaks.
2. Possession
"What you have"		 Hardware Keys (YubiKey) or Smartphones. Extremely strong because physical geographical theft is required to compromise it.
3. Inherence
"What you are"		 Fingerprints, FaceID, or DNA. The ultimate absolute assurance factor tying digital identity natively to your real-world biological presence.
