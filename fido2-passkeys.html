🧠 The WebAuthn (FIDO2) Guarantee
1. Zero Passwords Over The Air The server stores no passwords—only Public Keys. If the server's database is breached, the hackers steal literally nothing they can use to log into your account!
2. Phishing-Proof Origins The cryptographic signature is strictly tied to the URL in the browser's address bar. A hardware key physically cannot sign a login request for evil-bank.com using the key it made for bank.com.
3. Keys Stay Locked The Private Key is generated inside the hardware chip (YubiKey or Phone Enclave). It never leaves the device, cannot be copied, and cannot be extracted via software viruses.
📖 Analogy: The Digital Notary
Imagine the Server sends you a blank, randomly numbered check (the Challenge).

You take it to an un-bribeable Notary Public (the YubiKey). The Notary checks your physical ID (user tap), writes down the exact address of the building you are standing in (the Domain Origin), stamps it with their un-copyable metal seal (the Private Key Signature), and hands it back.

The bank accepts the check because the seal proves it wasn't forged, and the written address proves you were standing in the real bank, not a fake replica next door.
