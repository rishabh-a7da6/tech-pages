🛡️ Public Key Infrastructure (PKI)
The Certificate (SSL) An explicitly generated digital document cryptographically binding a server's public key dynamically mapping to a specific domain name (like exactly google.com).
The Digital Notary (CA) Highly trusted explicit "Notary" organizations globally responsible for natively issuing cryptographic stamps onto servers validating firm domain ownership.
Trusted Root Vault Your computer natively installs OS-level list of CA signatures it trusts. If a website's signature traces back to this list, the padlock securely turns Green.
