🛡️ Security & Protocol Breakdown
Direct Key Uploads
The old, dangerous way		 Solves the immediate access issue but permanently leaves your root identity tokens completely exposed at rest on intermediary servers. Never do this.
Agent Forwarding -A
Decent improvement		 Connects the Bastion directly to your laptop's memory via socket. Flaw: If the bastion is hacked while you are logged in, the hacker can natively hijack your active tunnel to forge signatures!
ProxyJump -J
The modern gold standard		 Creates a hermetically sealed, end-to-end TLS tunnel straight through to the Internal DB. The Bastion is fully relegated to a blind TCP forwarder, locking out attackers entirely.
