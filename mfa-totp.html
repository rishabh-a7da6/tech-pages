🧮 TOTP Algorithm Breakdown
The Cryptomath HMAC(SecretKey, EpochTime)
SecretKey (Seed) A base32 encoded string generated once securely during setup and stored internally on both hardware devices.
EpochTime (The Counter) The current Unix Unix timestamp divided strictly by 30. This ensures a brand new, discrete matching integer is targeted exactly twice a minute.
The Air Gap Advantage Because the math completes entirely locally, hackers cannot intercept codes via SMS SIM-swapping or Wi-Fi sniffing.
